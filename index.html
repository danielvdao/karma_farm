Karma Farm

An Android application by:
Daniel Dao
Andy Fan
Samuel Tipton

Karma Farm is a special Reddit client made specifically for racking up karma.

Front-page subreddits

Browse the most popular posts from all of Reddit's hand-picked default front-page subreddits. No login required!

Supports account persistent login

Want to do more than just browse? Access account-exclusive features, by logging into your reddit account directly within the app. Once logged in, Karma Farm will continue to remember your account until you log out.

Post viewing and voting

Post viewing and voting optimized for touch devices! Simply tap a post to view it conveniently within the app. Like a post? Swipe to the left to send your kudos in the form of an upvote. Alternatively, swipe a post to the right to bury it.

Karma Potential

Want to know where to comment to get maximize exposure, and more importantly, maximum karma? The higher the Karma Potential, the higher chance you can reap that delicious comment karma. Don't forget to say something funny or witty!

Comment voting and replying

Once you've found the perfect place to comment, reply directly by tapping the reply button. Just like posts, Karma Farm supports voting on comments.

Considerations

Developed as a part of Michael Scott's CS371M Mobile Computing course at the University of Texas, Austin. Source code available on Github.
